Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2025

Welcome to QR Lite. Your privacy is not just a feature; it is our core philosophy. This document explains how we protect your data while providing a world-class scanning experience.

1. Zero Data Collection (Offline-First)

QR Lite is built as an Offline-First application. This means:

  • No Cloud Storage: We do not have servers. Your scan history is stored exclusively on your device's local memory.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your location, your name, or what you scan.
  • No Accounts: You do not need to log in. You remain 100% anonymous.

2. Camera Permissions

The App requires Camera Access to function. This permission is used strictly for decoding QR codes and Barcodes in real-time. We do not record video, take photos, or upload any visual data to the internet.

3. Third-Party Services (Google AdMob)

To keep this App free, we use Google AdMob for advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device identifiers (like the Android Advertising ID) to serve relevant ads. You can opt-out of personalized ads in your Android Settings menu.

4. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally, its security depends on your device. If you delete the App or clear your phone's cache, your scan history will be permanently erased. We cannot recover it for you.
